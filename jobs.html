Position 2

Job Title: Junior Systems Administrator

Reference Number: 19v29

Employment Type: Casual – Full Time

Salary: $25–35 per hour

Join a leading Managed IT Services and Cybersecurity provider, where you’ll design, deploy, configure, and optimize resilient server and network infrastructures for enterprise clients across finance, healthcare, and retail sectors.

This position is tailored for technically‑driven individuals eager to implement automation scripts, monitor system performance metrics, and collaborate with senior engineers on critical IT and cybersecurity initiatives.

Role Summary

  • Administer user accounts, permissions, and security policies.
  • Deploy and configure virtual machines, cloud resources, and network devices.
  • Monitor system health and performance metrics using industry‑standard tools.
  • Automate routine tasks via scripting (PowerShell, Bash).
  • Provide escalation support and documentation for complex incidents.

Is this Role for You?

  1. You have completed or are pursuing relevant IT certifications (CompTIA A+, MCSA, or AWS) and can demonstrate your knowledge through academic projects, personal labs, or professional experience.
  2. You thrive in dynamic environments where you proactively identify areas for improvement, propose technical solutions, and champion automation initiatives that enhance service delivery and reduce mean time to resolution.
  3. You excel at collaborating with cross‑functional teams, translating business requirements into technical designs, and communicating complex concepts to non‑technical stakeholders with clarity and professionalism.

Qualifications

  • Experience with Active Directory, DNS, DHCP and Group Policy administration.
  • Basic proficiency in virtualization platforms (Hyper‑V, VMware) and cloud services (AWS, Azure).
  • Familiarity with ITIL frameworks and ticketing systems (e.g., ServiceNow).
  • Fundamental scripting skills (PowerShell, Bash) for automation and reporting.
  • Excellent communication, documentation, and troubleshooting abilities.
