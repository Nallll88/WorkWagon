Company Introduction


Welcome to WorkWagon — a streamlined recruitment platform designed to simplify how companies connect with top talent. Our full-stack web app, powered by Next.js, Prisma, and PostgreSQL, is built for speed, scalability, and ease of use. WorkWagon empowers employers to post jobs efficiently and enables job seekers to apply with confidence and clarity. We're focused on practical results: fewer barriers, smarter tools, and better hiring outcomes for everyone.

Company Details

  • Technology Stack: Built using Next.js, Prisma ORM, and PostgreSQL.
  • Primary Users: Employers looking to hire and job seekers seeking opportunities.
  • Key Features: Job postings, candidate dashboard, resume uploads, and secure user authentication.
  • Hosting & Deployment: Deployed via Vercel, Railway, or Docker for flexibility and performance.

Company Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Release the MVP (Minimum Viable Product) with essential job posting and application features.
  • Phase 2: Refine user interface and accessibility for both employers and applicants.
  • Phase 3: Add advanced features like saved jobs, resume uploads, and smart filtering.
  • Phase 4: Expand with analytics, admin tools, and paid promotional job listings.
